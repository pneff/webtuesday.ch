We meet at 19:30. Sensational AG, Giesshübelstrasse 62c, 8045 Zürich

Agenda

We'll have a look at the Free Software RDBMS PostgreSQL of which Version 9.2 has just been released. We'll see how to easily get it running and how it compares to other free RDBMS out there.

Aside of looking at some of the new stuff, we'll see how nice it is to have access to some of the awesome features in Postgres like transactional DDL, or lock-free ALTER TABLE's. Oh and did you know that you can run JavaScript right in your database?

Speaker

Philip Hofstetter (@pilif) is the lead developer at Sensational AG and is a huge fan of Postgres ever since migrating away from MySQL in the year 2000.

He loves talking about Databases, Unicode and email routing of all things
