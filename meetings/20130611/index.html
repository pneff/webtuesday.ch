7 languages in 49 minutes

  • We know that your favorite programming language is the best one and there is no doubt in that.
  • but, can you give our speakers 7 minutes to inspire you into changing the perspective.
  • or, discuss about why would you change perspective before all and how it can benefit you and your organization.

Each of 7 speakers will have just 7 minutes to present you the selected essentials of a programming language and the culture that surrounds it. Putting such a hard constraint on time will hopefully lead speakers to extracting the most crucial elements of their passion and why would you be interested in getting your share of it.

Speakers

  • Javascript - Jonas Wagner
  • Scala - Leo Büttiker
  • Go - Thomas Kappler
  • Scratch - Harry Fuecks
  • Python - Patrice Neff
  • Erlang - André Fatton
  • Haskell - Ivan Jovanovic
