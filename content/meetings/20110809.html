--- date: 2011-08-09 title: August 09, 2011 kind: meeting abstract: bitcoinJS - Money, Danger and JavaScript ---

We meet at 19:30 at Liip, Feldstrasse 133.

Agenda

BitcoinJS is a set of open-source libraries designed to let you quickly realize your custom Bitcoin project. Bitcoin is a peer-to-peer currency and has been called the "most dangerous open source project in history", so this promises to be interesting in a number of ways.

Lead developer Stefan Thomas will introduce the underlying concepts and give an in-depth tour of the project and its use of server-side JavaScript with node.js and MongoDB.
